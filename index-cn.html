About Me.

I'm a PhD student of Stepan S. Tsirkin in Titus Neupert's condensed matter theory group at the University of Zurich in Switzerland.

I am interested in first-principle study of various crystals which special properties. Recently I focus on simulating nonlinear transport phenomenons using wannier interpolation and Berry-Boltzmann equations. We are developing WannierBerri which is a code to calculate different properties by means of Wannier interpolation: Berry curvature, orbital moment and derived properties. Combined with simulations and experiments, I hope to understand more physics about nonlinear transports real materials.
